Never give any personal info to another pen-e-pal through your email

The Pen-E-Pals site will never ask for your address, password, or phone number. You should never give any personal info to anyone from this site.

Any posts where the interests are hateful, inappropriate or mean will be removed!

Contact Site Admin To report any abuse hostility or errors.

In this time of isolation and quarantine wouldn't it be nice to have an email pen pal to chat about the happy things that are still in our world?

That is the purpose of this site! No account needed, just enter your name, your preferred email, and some things that interest you. Anyone can reach out over email and strike up a conversation.
